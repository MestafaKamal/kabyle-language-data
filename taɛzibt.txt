Asefru-agi, "Anda-tt TEɛZIBT" yura-t umedyaz-acennay Lwennas  Maɛtub, icna-t-id d tizlit di tesfift "  S yisem n wayla-w " i d-yeffɣen deg useggas 1997. D tizlit yettwassnen aṭas sɣur acennay-agi imi heddṛen akk medden fell-as, aṭas i wumi teɛjeb nezzeh.

Ihi, deg usefru-agi, itterra-d umedyaz iman-is d usfih, d unguf neɣ d ccmata gar wid t-icban. Iqqaṛ-d akk ayen n dir i keṛhen yimdanen d wayen akken ur ilaq ara yakk ad ittwaxdem.

Syinn, ad nfaq d akken annect-agi i nettwali ɣer kra yimdanen, d netta i yesɛan azal di kra n teswayin, d netta i wumi ssalayen ccan. D yiwen seg waṭṭanen n tmetti i ɣef d-yewwi Lwennas awal deg tmedyazt-is.
Acewwiq

    Yak nnan: telha nniya
    D lḥila n ṣṣfa
    Bab-is, xas yeɣli, ad yekker
    Te ak udem i taggara
    Te ṛebbi lqima
    Winn -isɛan ur inegger
    Nekk ay atma ur iyi-tehwa
    Armi d-nemseggra
    I d-yendeh s yisem-iw liser

Asefru-agi ibeddu-d s yifyar-agi d-nsers dagi ufella. Ibɣa umedyaz ad ilin  d tazwert i wayen ara d-yini yakk deg usefru ines, s yis-sen ara ikcem deg usentel. Ittmeslay-d ɣef xayen akk ilhan di nniya, am deg wayen iɛeddan am deg wayen d-iteddun.

    Yak nnan: telha nniya
    D lḥila n ṣṣfa
    Bab-is, xas yeɣli, ad yekker

Issexdem tanfalit " yak nnan " i wakken ad d-yini d aken d annect-a ittwalin yimdanen, d annect-a i d-nnan wat zik ɣef nniya akked wayen i yelhan deg-s ladɣa ṣṣfa akked tnekkra yall mi ara yeɣli umdan. Ad nger tamawt belli awal illa-d ɣef yizri d wayen ssnen yimdanen ɣef nniya.

    Tettak udem i taggara
    Tettṛebbi lqima
    Winn tt-isɛan ur inegger

IIhi yuɣal umedyaz ittmeslay-d ɣef wurmir, neɣ ayen d-iteddun, abeɛda taggara icebḥen. Am winn ara ak-ikksen aɣilif mi ara ak-yini : "  Winn tt-isɛan ur inegger ", acku  imdaden akk ur bɣin ara ad negren. Bɣan akk ad d-ttwabedren, bɣan akk ad ddren deg wulawen  n wiyaḍ xas ma mmuten. Ihi dagi amedyaz iṛgem-asen i wat nniya tudert nniḍen akked lqima yugaren deffir lmut nsen.

    Nekk ay atma ur iyi-tehwa
    Armi d-nemseggra
    I d-yendeh s yisem-iw liser

Dagi i yegzem Lwennas amrar segmi t-yesseɣzef. Yuɣal ittmeslay-d ɣef yiman-is i yufan liser asmi i d-yemseggra akked nniya. Da netta, issexdem tanfalit "  indeh-d s yisem-iw " akken ad d-iglem amek i d-ldin fell-as tewwura ɣer wayen ilhan.

Ibɣa si tama nniḍen ad d-ijbed tameẓẓuɣt n wid, am netta, i yeğğan nniya i wakken ad  ten-isselheḍ s wawal-is, i wakken daɣen ad lḥun akken deg ubrid n liser beṛṛa n win n nniya.
Izli amenzu

    Anda-tt teɛzibt ur neṛdiḥ
    Neɣ asebsi ur nendiḥ
    S Lḥesnawi d Lɛenqa
    Selseɣ-as i lɣerḍ jjiḥ
    Ass-a a d-tettiqsiḥ
    Tedwilt-nni ur ṛğiɣ ara
    Yak nnan: leɛmeṛ yerbiḥ
    Bnadem ur neqbiḥ
    D lweqt ad niqbiḥ tura
    ɣer tḥeṛci ad d-ncerreg ttriḥ
    Ad asen-neḍlu amsiḥ
    I wid isburren nniya

Wagi d izli amezwaru n tezlit. Deg-s ara d-iban yilɣi n usefru, yerna deg-s daɣen ara d-yefk umedyaz tamuɣli talqayant tamezwarut ɣef wayen d-yebda di tezwart ines. Nniya-nni i yeğğa di tezwara, ad d-yini ayen tura i tt-yeğğa ad yernu ad d-yeglem ayen i t-issawḍen ɣer ubrid-agi amaynut i d-yewwi.

Gar yimdanen akk ukkud ara d-yeddu umedyaz, llan yiɛziben neɣ tiɛzibin i d-yejbed dagi srid ɣer tezlit-is.

    Anda-tt teɛzibt ur neṛdiḥ
    Neɣ asebsi ur nendiḥ
    S Lḥesnawi d Lɛenqa

Ittmeslay-d ladɣa ɣef yimɛawazen ar sḥuṛ, wid yessedhay uẓawan alamma d tisselbi, wid yeṛdaḥen s Lḥesnawi akked Lɛenqa, wid daɣen isɛeddayen uḍan nsen seg usebsi ɣer wayeḍ. Idda-d akkid-sen akken ad icrek yid-sen tameddurt akked tmuɣli nsen, yerna ad ibded ɣer-sen imi ula d netta akken i iga.

Syinn yeqqel iheddeṛ-d ɣef wayen t-issagaden d wayen i yis ittqabal yir tiswayin. Inna-as deg wawal-is:

    Selseɣ-as i lɣerḍ jjiḥ
    Ass-a a d-tettiqsiḥ
    Tedwilt-nni ur ṛğiɣ ara

Imi ala jjiḥ i asen-d iggran i yimdanen t-icban mi ara d-tettiqsiḥ yir taswayt, mi ara ten-id-teḥṛes yir tedwilt, ulayɣer amennuɣ, ulayɣer ssbeṛ, ulayɣer nnif. Ala jjiḥ kan i d-yeggran ad yefru yis-s umdan timsal-is, acku s jjiḥ, ayen i as-ihwan ad t-ixdem, ayen i as-ihwan ad yaweḍ ɣer-s.

Syin, ad ixdem ugar waya, ad isenned ɣer yiwen n yinzi yettwassnen mliḥ deg yidles aqbayli, abeɛda "  Leɛmeṛ yerbiḥ bnadem ur neqbiḥ " i wakken ad iserreḥ yakk i tmasit (responsabilité) ines, ad yeğğ ɣer  deffir ayen t-icudden ɣer tmetti akked wayen i t-yurzen deg-s.

Imdaden iban ad uɣalen ad t-ttwalin s yir tamuɣli, ad t-keṛhen, ur t-ttamnen ara. D annect-n ara ismeɣṛen tiḥḥeṛci ines. Ad yuɣal ad d-izzi, di taggara, ɣef wid isɛan nniya i wakken, s jjiḥ-is, ad asen-ikkes ayen akken ibɣa neɣ ad iɛfes deg yizerfan nsen i wakken ad issemɣer ayla-s akken i as-yehwa.
Izli wiss sin

    S rrba i d iyi-ssrebḥen
    Idim yemmaren
    D aneɛyab ttwargameɣ
    Ṣedden lerbug akk celxen
    Ula i d iyi-d-yeṭṭfen
    Deg uḍṛef n ugni n yiseɣ
    Allen n zik yettneḥcamen
    Ur ttadrent dayen
    Xas ma zdat wi ttqadaṛeɣ
    Tagmat ad as-rrẓeɣ ifadden
    Ad as-gerrzeɣ lekfen
    Deg uzabuq ad as-serrḥeɣ

Ikka-d Lwennas di tmeddurt seg waṭas n yir tiswayin i ibeddlen tudert-is. Aṭas n tikkal i deg yettwet yerna  gaddac i yuḍen. Ihi dagi, imi d-yerra iman-is deg umur n ccmata, issexdem rrba-nni yakk i d-iɣlin fell-as, idammen-is yemmaren akked yir leɛyub i d-yeggran di tfekka-s i wakken ad d-issebded yis-sen tamuɣli-agi ines tamaynut.

    ɣef waya i d-as-yenna:
    S rrba i d iyi-ssrebḥen
    Idim yemmaren
    D aneɛyab ttwargameɣ

Maci kan d rrba i d-iɣlin fell-as, d idammen-is i yemmaren neɣ d leɛyub i as-d-iggran, lameɛna yerna rregmat s ufella. Ittwet di tfekka-as yerna yettwajṛeḥ deg wul-is. Ihi, issenfalay-d abeɛda urfan ines mgal ayen yakk i yeɛeddan fell-as. Ibɣa daɣen ad isfeḍ ɣer-sen jjiḥ-is. D imi ara icerreg akk lerbug i t-yeṭṭfen ɣer tmetti neɣ ɣer yiseɣ, ladɣa mi d as-inna:

    Ṣedden lerbug akk celxen
    Ula i d iyi-d-yeṭṭfen
    Deg uḍṛef n ugni n yiseɣ

Syinn daɣen, am deg yizli amenzu, ad ixdem ugar mi ara yekkes akk leḥya seg yiman-is. ɣef waya, ad yuɣal ur issadar ara akk allen-is am zik-nni zdat winn i yettqadaṛ ama d imeqqṛanen, ama d alag-is, ama d imawlan-is…, abeɛda mi as-yenna:

    Allen n zik yettneḥcamen
    Ur ttadrent dayen
    Xas ma zdat wi ttqadaṛeɣ

Yerna-d syinn kra yifyar nniḍen i deg i d-yessenfal daɣen ayen n dir ara yexdem. Awala-agi aneggaru, isɛa aṭas n yinumak akken d-qqaṛen yimdanen. Yall yiwen amek d-issefhem acu i yebɣa ad t-id-yini umedyaz.

    Tagmat ad as-rrẓeɣ ifadden
    Ad as-gerrzeɣ lekfen
    Deg uzabuq ad as-serrḥeɣ

S tmuɣli tamezwarut, ad d-nefhem d akken, amedyaz ad isres seg yiri-s tagmat, ad yuɣal am akken ur ittamen ara akk yis-s imi netta ur isɛi ara gma-s. Ad ittmuqul kan amek ara tt-yerreẓ neɣ ad tt-isfeḍ seg wulawen i wakken ad d-ggrin yimdanen akk d iwḥiden am netta. Yall winn isɛan gma-s ad t-inkeṛ, ad  t-ideqqeṛ am akken ur t-id-sɛin ara yakk yimawlan-is.

Amedyaz ibɣa ad ixdem annect-a acku tagmat d yiwen n uzamul seg yizumal n yiseɣ akked nnif akked niyya. Segmi akken i yeğğa nniya, ad yeğğ akk ayen d-teğğa.

Illa unamek nniḍen i d-ttadren yimdanen, nsell-as s waṭas ɣer yilmeẓyen akked yimɛawazen ar sḥuṛ. Qqaṛen-d d akken awal-agi igellem-d deg-s umedyaz amek ttkeyyifen yimdanen. Ad issefruri iferr n ccemma, syinn ad t-yerr di lkaɣeḍ amellal (i wumi iqqar dagi lekfen acku d amellal daɣen), dɣa mi ara t-ikeyyef, amzun akken ikcem di tudert nniḍen neɣ d azabuq i t-yewwin mbaɛid ɣef tillawt.

Di tidet, ur nezṛi ara anwa i d anamek n tidet i yebɣa ad t-id-issiweḍ umedyaz. A wi yufan di sin yinumak ṣeḥḥan, axateṛ nessutur akk assad yili usefru isɛa aṭas n yinumak deg yiwet n tikklet. D annect-n i as-yettaken azal i usefru neɣ i tutlayt i deg yettwura.
Izli wiss kṛad

    Ṣṣifa n leḥbab ad tt-beddleɣ
    S tḥeṛci ad tt-jeggreɣ
    S tumlilt tmenqas-iw
    Bu ttrika ɣeṛ-s ad senndeɣ
    Ma d wayeḍ ad t-ḥeqṛeɣ
    Xas yefruri-d seg uzaṛ-iw
    ɣer Rebbi, afus ad t-id-kkseɣ
    Aḥezzeb ad t-nefḍeɣ
    Ad t-nfuɣ seg yidisan-iw
    At zzuṛ, ad ten-id-necdeɣ
    S yis-sen ad nefḍeɣ
    Widen ur icekkeb lɣerḍ-iw

Ibeddel yidis n tmuɣli n umedyaz deg yizli-agi. Segmi yella ittmeslay-d ɣef tudert-is netta akked wayen ara ibeddel akk deg yiman-is, yeqqel tura ɣer tmetti akked umkan-is gar yimadanen d wayen ara ixdem gar-s akkid-sen.

Am akken d-yemmeslay ɣef tegmat, dagi ad d-izwir seg yimdukkal-is neɣ si tdukkli s umata. Ihi, ad yuɣal ad yeğğ tadukkli, ad ideqqeṛ akk imeddukkal-is, ad ikkes nniya i yesɛa ɣer-sen. Syinn ad d-tegri ala tiḥeṛci akked tmenqas. Inna-as:

    Ṣṣifa n leḥbab ad tt-beddleɣ
    S tḥeṛci ad tt-jeggreɣ
    S tumlilt tmenqas-iw

Ad yeğğ akk imeddukkal-is iqdimen, ad ten-id-ibeddel s wiyaḍ, s yimdanen i ten-yifen neɣ wid i ɣer ara yaf ayen ilhan d wayen t-iseɛden.

Imdanen-agi i ɣer ara yezzi d wid isɛan ttrika neɣ idrimen axater d nitni ara yaf asmi ara ixaṣṣ, d nitni ara as-d-ifken asmi ara yeḥwiğ asuṛdi. Ma d leḥbab-is, xas ammer ah-at ttilin-as neɣ twacult-is, ad asen-ibru acku ur yelli kra ara d-irbeḥ sɣur-sen.

Syinn ad d-yaf iman-is umedyaz ala ɣef yiman-is kan yerna ala i yiman-is kan. Ur d-ucliɛ deg yiwen, ur as-d-tuqa lmeɛna di kra nniḍen ixḍan taqeṛṛuct-is. Ad yidir tudert-is s tḥeṛci akked tmenqas, ad d-issukkes iman-is seg wakken akk yella zik-nni i wakken ayen akk ur izmir yakan ad t-ixdem, tura ad as-yizmir. Ad iteddu kan deffir lɣeṛḍ-is alamma yewweḍ ɣer-s.

ɣef wannect-a, d-ikkes afus-is si Rebbi, acku tikkwal d tagdi n Rebbi i d-ittaṭṭafen amdan ur ixeddem ara ayen i ɣer t-issendeh yiman-is. D tagdi n Rebbi i yessawaḍen amdan ad issusem tikkwal, ad yeğğ si lḥeqqiq i wakken ad yili lḥeqq n wayeḍ. D acu kan, dagi, amedyaz i ur ittḥezzib ara yakk i tigi, ad teddu kan deffir lebɣi-s.

Yerna, ad d-inced ɣer ɣur-s ala wid ara t-iɛiwnen ad yaweḍ sani yettnadi. abeɛda at zzuṛ neɣ imdanen issawalen tikerkas, wid iteffren tidet i wakken d awal nsen kan ara iɛeddin, neɣ d ayen bɣan kann ara yilin ledɣa ma yella tidet-nni yellan ur teddi ara akkid-sen.

    At zzuṛ, ad ten-id-necdeɣ
    S yis-sen ad nefḍeɣ
    Widen ur icekkeb lɣerḍ-iw

Izli wiss ukkuẓ

    D usfih i yesburren ṛṛxa
    Yeḍsan af lḥeṛma
    I iferrun tilla ass-agi
    D ungif yeččan deg uɣenja
    Uɣur d-teggra tɣawsa
    Af yiderman a d-ittṛayi
    Taddert mi ara tḥemmel zzayla
    Seg yimezwura
    I as-d-ttḥuccun mebla acetki
    Ma tesseɛteb-ik tmara
    Zuf beddel tama
    Tettuḍ amek iga yiḍelli

Ha-t-ah yizli izaden akk deg usefru-a, acku dagi ibeddel akk umedyaz tamuɣli ines akked yidis-is. Ittmeslay-d tura ɣef wayen t-iqeṛḥen s tidet akked wayen i ɣef i t-iɣaḍ lḥal di tmetti. Limmer ad nessikked akken ilha yakk asefru-agi amek i iga, ad nwali d akken armi d taggara-agi i d-iqqaṛ ayen i as-yersen ɣef wul akked wayen i t-issawḍen yakk ad yaru asefru ines. Timsal-agi t-iqeṛhen d tid nettwali si tama ɣer tayeḍ di tmetti.

    D usfih i yesburren ṛṛxa
    Yeḍsan af lḥeṛma
    I iferrun tilla ass-agi

D ungif, amdan-nni ur nga leqdeṛ i yima-is, d winna i ɣer d-iggra udebbeṛ, d netta ah-at i d ameqṛan n taddert, d netta i bab n ṛṛay i ttabaɛen akk yimdanen. Ay akken d-inna ad xedmen.

Tamawt kan d akken amedyaz isseqdec tanfalit " Ičča deg uɣenja ", acku di tmeɣṛa, neɣ deg yimensi n yiɛeğğalen, učči seg uɣenja d lɛib ameqqṛan. Am akkenni winn ara yeččen seg uɣenja, d ccyaḍ i icaḍ neɣ ur issin ara ilugan n yimensi neɣ n lmakla. Ibɣa ad aɣ-d-issiweḍ umedyaz tikti ines ɣef yimdanen-agi i iteṭṭfen ṛṛay, amek gan d wamek i ten-ittwali, abeɛda d wid ur neswi ara yakk, neɣ d wid ur nesɛi ara yakk azal.

D tigi i d timsal i t-iqeṛṛhen. D annect-a yakk i iswi n usefru-agi. D acu kan, maci d tiḥḥeṛci nsen i yessawḍen imdanen-agi ad ṭṭfen ṛṛay neɣ ad debbṛen, neɣ d tamussni i izaden ɣer-sen. D agdud s yiman-is, i mezdaɣ n taddert, neɣ d timetti s umata i asen-ifkan azal. Isseqdec da netta yiwen n yinzi yettwasnen deg yidles aqbayli:

    Taddert mi ara tḥemmel zzayla
    Seg yimezwura
    I as-d-ttḥuccun mebla acetki

D imdanen s yiman nsen, d nitni i yessulin ccan n wungifen, i yefkan azal i wasfihen. D nitni i yerran amusnaw di rrif, d nitni i yessawḍen amdan ilhan ad iɛzel iman-is acku ur izmir ara ad ihdeṛ zdat wid i wumi ɣezzif yiles.

Ihi akk i ɣef i d-yemmeslay umedeyaz si tezwara, ur yelli ara d ayen i yebɣa ad t-ixdem netta. Iwwi-t-id kan d awal ɣef yimdanen-agi ur nesɛi iseɣ wala nniya, yerna d nitni i yuɣalen d iqeṛṛa ɣef ugdud.

Di teswayt am tagi, winn ifehmen neɣ winn isɛan allaɣ-is yers, ur izmir ara ad iqabel imdanen-agi. Ur izmir ara ad d-isres azal-agi i asen-tefka tmetti. Teggra-d kan i umdan ad yinig ad ibeddel tama, ad iṛuḥ anda akken maci d lbaṭel i yeṭṭfen ṛṛay.

    Ma tesseɛteb-ik tmara
    Zuf beddel tama
    Tettuḍ amek iga yiḍelli

Tamsirt

Tamsirt i ilaq ad tt-iṭṭef umdan seg usefru-a d tamsirt ẓẓayen aṭas. Ayen i ɣef i d-yewwi Lwennas awal dagi, d yiwen n waṭṭan ameqqṛan i yellan di tmetti. Mi ara yili yir bnadem i wumi teggen medden ccan, yerna d amdan bu yiseɣ i tterran di rrif.

Ihi, amedyaz inna-d akk ayen xeddmen yimdanen-agi, usfihen-agi, ineggura-agi di tmetti. Ihdeṛ-d yakk ɣef txidas nsen akked tmenqas nsen akked wayen sɛan diri-t. Iglem-d daɣen amek ttwalin ddunit d wayen bɣan ad awḍen ɣer-s.

Syin, isummel-d d akken maci d nitni i yewwḍen sani wwḍen, maca d timetti s yiman-is i ten-irefden. Rran ayen n dir ilha, syinn rran ayen ilhan diri-t. Am akken i as-yenna Mulud Zedek deg wawal-is:

    Ilha wayen ilhan
    Diri-t wayen n dir
    Ma diri ayen ilhan
    Ad ilhu wayen n dir

Tazemmurt ittewten, d nettat i ifkan afus i tqabact. Aṭas n yiɣeblan neɣ n waṭṭanen, d timetti i ten-id-iwwin i yiman-is, d nettat i ten-id-iqnen i yiri-s. D nettat i yerran deg yidis ayen yelhan deg-s i wakken ad yeggri kan wayen n dir.

Imdanen ilhan, wid isɛan azal akked yiseɣ, ah-at ur d-ttbanen ara akk. D widak-nni kan tt-isserkayen i d-ittbanen, d nitni i iqeddcen, d nitni i yettwassnen, d nitni yuɣalen ferrun timsal s ṛṛay nsen uzlig.

A wi yukan, yall taɣawsa ad teqqel s amekkan-is. D amusnaw ara yemmlen abrid i watma-s, d aḥeddad n wawal ara iḍebbṛen ɣef yimdanen, d bu yiseɣ ara nesbedd ad iḥkem, d bu nniya ara neg d amalal ad t-namen, acku d nitni d tidet i yessnen d acu d yewwi lḥal. Am akken as-yenna:

    D winn i neqqcen tibḥirt
    I yezṛan akal amek iga

Ma d amdan n dir, ilaq ad as-nemmel anda akken isxaṣṣ. Ur ilaq ad as-neg ccan, ur ilaq ad nesxaṣṣ di lqima-s. Am netta am yimdanen nniḍen, ayen i deg yessen, ad as-nefk udem, ma d anda ur yessin, ilaq ad as-nwahhi abrid n ṣṣwab.

    Ay ansi d-yessawel lḥeqq
    Abrid-is ad iyi-yawi

Tuggra

Limmer maci d imezwura, ur ttilin yineggura. Asentel-agi, aṭas n yinazuṛen i icnan fell-as am Mulud Zedek, Sliman  ɛazem neɣ Lewnis At Mengellat… D acu kan, Lwennas Maɛtub, ixtaṛ ad d-yerr iman-is deg umekkan n yir imdanen-agi i wakken d isseqdec tazmert n wawal-is deg uglam n tikta-agi tiberkanin.

Imdanen akk ɣilen d akken di tezlit-agi, Lwennas yuɣal d yir amdan, d ccmata neɣ d usfih. D acu kan, amdan ilaq ad ifhem timsal. Mi d-yerra i yerra iman-is akka umedyaz, maci i wakken ad issiweḍ imdanen ad xedmen am netta, maci i wakken i infeḍ iseɣ akked nniya, maci i wakken ad ibru i tjaddit, lameɛna isɛa deg wallaɣ-is d akken ala s wannect-a ara d-ijbed tameẓẓuɣt n yimdanen i wakken ad as-iḥessen.

Ihi Lwennas ur ibri i nniya, ur ikkes leḥya, ur ideqqeṛ imeddukkal, ur ittekka ɣer bu ttrika, ur iḥqiṛ igellil, ur d-ikkis afus-is si Ṛebbi, ur… Annect-a yakk maci d netta i t-ixeddmen. Maca wid t-ixeddmen,  ḍemɛen d akken yis-s ara alin…
